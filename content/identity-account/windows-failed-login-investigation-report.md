Alert Summary</strong></p><p>Wazuh detected a brute force attack against the local Windows machine APARNAA on June 11, 2026. Twenty consecutive failed login attempts targeting a non-existent account FakeAttacker were recorded within approximately 7 seconds. The speed and volume of attempts - far beyond human typing capability - confirm automated tool-driven activity. Wazuh rule 60204 (Multiple Windows Logon Failures) fired at severity level 10, automatically correlating the individual failures into a brute force alert and mapping the activity to MITRE ATT&amp;CK T1110.</p><p><strong>2.
